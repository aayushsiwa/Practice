1.Let's make a really Healthy Brussel Sprout Salad

Ingredients

  • 5 cups brussel sprout, shaved
  • 1/5 cup olive oil
  • 1/5 cup lemon juice
  • 1 cup roasted walnut
  • 1/2 dried berries of your choosing
  • 1/3 cup ricotta cheese shaved
  • Salt and Pepper

    • Directions

  • Slice the brussel sprout using mandoline, set aside
  • Wash the sliced brussel sprout, and let it dry with vegetable drier
  • Mix brussel sprout, lemon juice, walnut, dried berries, and cheese into a bowl and toss it
  • Drizzle with olive oil

    • And there you have it... Healthy Brussel Sprout Salad
